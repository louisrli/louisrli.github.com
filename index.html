About

I’m an undergraduate at Harvard University. I’m currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematical Sciences. This site has my sideprojects and occasional blog posts.

I have diverse interests in programming and computer science, among which are (but not limited to) human-computer interaction, application security, and mobile development. Last summer, I was an intern at Akiban, an amazing database startup in Boston. I’m currently an undergraduate researcher at the Intelligent Interactive Systems Group under Krzysztof Gajos. I’m also the managing developer of the iOS team at Rover.

I like mathematical programming puzzles and wargames.

Surprisingly, I do enjoy a few things unrelated to computers: running, bboying (breakdancing), and poker (particularly heads up no limit).
